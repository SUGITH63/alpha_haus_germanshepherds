Meet FINN - Our Champion Stud Dog

FINN - Our Champion Stud Dog

FINN is an exceptional male German Shepherd, known for his impressive pedigree, excellent temperament, and outstanding physical attributes. He is available for stud services to approved females.

  • Pedigree: FINN comes from a long line of champion German Shepherds with multiple titles and awards.
  • Temperament: FINN is calm, confident, and highly trainable. He has a gentle demeanor with family and a strong protective instinct.
  • Physical Attributes: FINN boasts a robust build, excellent conformation, and striking features, including a rich coat and expressive eyes.
  • Achievements: FINN has won several titles in dog shows and competitions, showcasing his superior qualities and training.

If you are interested in utilizing FINN for stud services, please contact us for more information on terms and availability.
